Levonorgestrel Breakdown

Levonorgestrel

Ethinyl Estradiol

Levonorgestrel (Progestin)

  • Prevents ovulation
  • Thickens cervical mucus
  • Maintains pregnancy prevention

Ethinyl Estradiol (Estrogen)

  • Stabilizes uterine lining
  • Works with progestin
  • Regulates menstrual cycle

How they work together:

These two hormones work as a team to prevent pregnancy. The progestin does most of the heavy lifting by stopping your ovaries from releasing eggs, while the estrogen helps keep your cycle regular and reduces breakthrough bleeding.
